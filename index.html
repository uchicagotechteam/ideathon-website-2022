UNIVERSITY of CHICAGO

TECHTEAM IDEATHON 2022

APRIL 22–24, 2022. JOIN US.

WHAT IS IT?

Join us for 3 days (April 22–24) where you and your team can identify and propose creative solutions to some of the major issues in tech and policy. These solutions could take the form of a government policy/tax/subsidy, app, program, educational class—anything! Absolutely feel free to think outside the box!

At the end of the Ideathon, your team will submit a slideshow presentation and a 3 minute recorded video of an elevator pitch for the problem you are addressing and your solution. We will provide detailed guidelines for what we are looking for in your slide deck.

WHO IS IT FOR?

Anyone who attends the University of Chicago! We want to create an open space for discussion and thoughtful collaboration across all majors and schools.

We are asking for teams of 5 students. At the time of registration, you may register as a team or as an individual. During the Ideathon, we will match individuals to form teams.

WHERE IS IT?

We have space booked in the Rubenstein Forum for speaker events and work sessions during the weekend.

TELL ME MORE

No technical or coding skills necessary! We encourage students with a variety of skills, interests, and backgrounds to sign up.
